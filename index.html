Welcome to Q4OS

Q4OS is a fast and powerful operating system built on the latest technologies, offering a highly productive desktop environment. Our focus is on security, reliability, long-term stability, and the conservative integration of verified new features. Q4OS stands out for its speed and very low hardware requirements, running smoothly on both brand-new machines and legacy computers. It is also well-suited for virtualization and cloud computing.
